About

Audrey Hasson is in the class of 2022 at Centennial High School, and completed this research during her sophomore year. Outside of research, she pretends to be Elle Woods on her Mock Trial team and plays french horn and soccer. Audrey listens to showtunes, likes to read, and is a proud owner of a Google Pixel.
